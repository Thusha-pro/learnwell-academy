Our Difference

Weekly examinations.
Genuine accountability.

Every student sits a structured assessment each week — marked rigorously by our tutors and reviewed together in class. Parents receive regular progress updates so there are no surprises come exam season.

  • Structured weekly tests Past-paper style questions on the week's topics, mirroring real exam conditions.
  • Detailed marking Each script returned with full working, comments, and improvement points.
  • Progress reviews One-to-one discussion of marks, common errors, and the path forward — every week.
  • Parent updates Regular reports keeping parents informed of trends, achievements, and areas of focus.
